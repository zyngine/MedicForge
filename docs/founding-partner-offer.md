## What is MedicForge?

MedicForge is an all-in-one platform for EMS training programs and agencies:

**For Training Programs (LMS):**
- Course management with modules, lessons, and video content
- Quiz builder with auto-grading and curve grading options
- NREMT competency tracking and skill checklists
- Clinical hours logging and patient contact documentation
- Student progress dashboards and analytics

**For Agencies (Compliance Portal):**
- Employee roster and certification tracking
- PA state competency library
- Annual verification cycles
- Medical director oversight with digital signatures
- Full audit trail for compliance
